## Notes

- **LOLA** appears to be a proprietary treatment or protocol name used in patient testimonials. No official expansion was found in the codebase.
- **CE** marking indicates compliance with European health, safety, and environmental protection standards for medical devices.
- **CNDP** is the Moroccan data protection authority, equivalent to a Data Protection Authority (DPA) in the EU.
- All medical acronyms (PRP, HIFU, DU, etc.) are kept in their original form across all three languages as they are internationally recognized medical terms.
